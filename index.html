Fitness freak studio is a cross fit station which has come up with a new concept of body designing. We have a number of workout routines such as CrossFit and Functional training, Pilates, Zumba and Aerial yoga, and many Cardiovascular workouts like Insanity, Cardio Kickboxing and many more with certified trainers who guide you to become your best self and help you achieve your fitness goals. We design a workout routine based on the person’s body type and their requirements, while also keeping in mind the person’s medical condition/ situation if any. We like to establish a perfect balance between a person’s body and mind.
