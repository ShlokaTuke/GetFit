CROSSFIT

CrossFit has constantly varying functional movements performed at high intensity that reflect the best aspects of gymnastics, weightlifting, running, rowing and more - the core movements of life! These movements move the largest loads over the longest distances and are thus ideal for maximizing the amount of calories burnt in the shortest time leading to dramatic improvements in fitness.


CrossFit challenges the world's fittest and has universal scalability, making it perfect for any committed individual, regardless of experience.


Calorie Burnt: Up to 1,000 calories.


Target Areas: Thighs, Arms, Glutes, Core & Back.


We have helped more than 500+ members acheive their fitness goals using crossfit.


Equipments Used:
    -Olympic Barbell
    -Bumper Plates
    -Pull-Up Bar/ Power Rack
    -Jump Rope and/ or Battle Rope
    -Gymnastic Rings
    -Kettlebells
    -Ploymetric Box/ Squat Box
    -Medicine Ball/ Slam Ball
    -Weight Bench
    -Glute Ham Developer (GHD)
    -Parallettes
    -Rowing Machine
