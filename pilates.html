PILATES

Pilates emphasizes on balanced development of the body through core strength & flexibility allowing efficient, graceful movement.


Exercise modification is the key to Pilates success. All exercises are developed with modifications that can make a workout safe and challenge a person at any level. Core strength and torso stability, along with the six Pilates principles - Centering, Control, Flow, Breath, Precision, and Concentration, is what sets the Pilates method apart from many other types of exercise.


Calorie Burnt: Up to 500+ calories.


Target Areas: Thighs, Arms, Glutes, Core & Back.


We have a large floor space for group sessions and also have pilates reformer bench, ladder barrel, wunda chair and spinal corrector that ensure complete pilates workouts.
