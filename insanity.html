INSANITY

Insanity is a total body workout that uses one's own body weight for resistance. It is based on "Max Interval Training" and has 3-minute periods of highest possible intensity, with a 30-second of rest in between. This increases aerobic fitness level while burning fat.


Calorie Burnt: Up to 1,000 calories.


Target Areas: Core, Arms, Legs Glutes & Back.


We have 10 workouts in the Insanity program, with each routine lasting between 30 to 60 minutes, 6 days a week. Over the last two years, we have conducted 1000+ insanity sessions, helping more than 700+ members lose a average 20+ kgs.
